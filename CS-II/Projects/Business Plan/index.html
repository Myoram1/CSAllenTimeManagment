Business Plan


Identity


Our ISP is an app that connects computer science students to teacher and tutors around the world.


Problem


Computer science students are constantly in need of asssistance with their work from a tutor. Without a reliable way to get help, they will fall behind in their classwork resulting in low grades and a low understanding in their work.

Solution

Bolt offers students a service that connects them with top tutors and professors to help them to complete and understand their computer science homework.


Target Market


Our target audience is computer science students.


Competition

Our competition is Merlin Maven Tutors.


Revenue Streams


Our main source of revenue would be a subscription service that our users would subscribibe to. It would be $3.99 a month.

Marketing Strategies


Our marketing activities would be buying ad space on Codermerlin, Stack Overflow, and other coding websites.


Expenses


Our expenses would be paying our tutors for their services.
