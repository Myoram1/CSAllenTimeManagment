Why did we decide to make this app?

We decided to make this app to improve study time, management, and productability in students. We wanted to create an app where you could input your assignments and work time estimates, and you are given a plan to help you reach those goals. With this in mind, we hope that these goals you are setting help increase your mental resiliance and help combat smartphone addiction.
